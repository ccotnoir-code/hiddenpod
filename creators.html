Questions

Frequently asked.

What is PodSignal and how is it calculated?
PodSignal is HiddenPod's proprietary quality scoring system. Every episode is assessed across six weighted dimensions: Audio Quality (18%), Content Structure (20%), Topic Relevance (22%), Clip-ability (16%), Consistency (16%), and Episode Vitality (8%). Episodes can also earn up to 10 bonus points through Discovery Momentum when outperforming a show's own baseline. The result is a score from 0–100 (plus momentum bonus) that reflects episode quality independent of audience size.
Is the free tier really free? What's the catch?
Completely free — no credit card required. The free tier gives you your Audio Quality score for your three most recent episodes, which is enough to understand one of the most impactful dimensions. Upgrade to Starter for the full six-dimension breakdown and rankings.
Which shows are eligible to claim?
Any active independent podcast not affiliated with a major network or appearing on platform top charts. If your show is in our database, you can claim it. If it isn't yet, use the claim flow to submit it for inclusion — we typically score new shows within 48 hours.
How does HiddenPod verify I own my show?
We send a verification email to the address associated with your podcast's RSS feed. If that isn't accessible, we offer an alternative verification via a short audio marker in your next episode.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes — cancel anytime from your dashboard. Your score and claimed show remain on file; you'll just revert to the free tier's Audio Quality dimension visibility.
