How it works

What is PodSignal?

PodSignal is HiddenPod's proprietary quality scoring system. Every active independent podcast is assessed across six dimensions — audio quality, content structure, topic relevance, clip-ability, consistency, and episode vitality. Scores run 0–100. The result is a score that tells listeners whether a show is worth their time, and tells creators exactly what to improve.

PodSignal measures production quality, structure, consistency, and topical fit. It does not evaluate, fact-check, or vouch for the accuracy of a show's content.

Audio QualityClarity, balance, mastering — 18%
Content StructureHook speed, flow, segment density — 20%
Topic RelevanceCategory alignment, title-content match — 22%
Clip-abilityQuotable moments, emotional range — 16%
ConsistencyCadence regularity, length stability — 16%
Episode VitalityTime-sensitive speed or evergreen durability — 8%
