How PodSignal rankings work

Every active independent podcast is scored weekly across six dimensions. Only shows not affiliated with major networks or appearing on platform top charts are eligible. Scores reflect episode quality, not audience size — a 200-listener show can rank above a 200,000-listener show on merit.
